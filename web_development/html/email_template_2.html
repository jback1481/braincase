care customer service experience!

We know health care can be complicated. So we’re changing things up to make it a whole lot easier.

Get ready for UnitedHealthcare Concierge, coming January 1, 2018!

One call does it all.

  • Looking for an in-network, high-quality provider? We’ll find one and even make your appointment.
  • Questions about an unexpected bill or claim? We’ll call your provider and figure it out. Once we know, we’ll call you back with answers.
  • Want to make sure you’re not overpaying for health care? We’ll explain what’s covered, compare costs for your local providers and review all your options on where to go for care.
  • FSA, HSA, HRA — not sure of the differences or how they work with your medical plan? We’ll explain the differences.
  • Dealing with a complex medical issue like autism, or a condition that requires specialty medications? We’ll answer any questions you may have and connect you to other helpful resources.
  • Want to create a plan to save for health care expenses today and in the future? We’ll look for ways you can save on your current expenses (e.g., switch to a generic medication) and look for long-term savings opportunities.

No jargon. No runaround. No kidding.

Starting January 1, your UnitedHealthcare Concierge will be a phone call away, Monday through Friday during business hours, to help with just about anything. We look forward to helping you! Stay tuned for details.
